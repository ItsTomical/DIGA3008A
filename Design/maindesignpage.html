Reference material

  • The Seth Hampton portfolio website is my main inspiration. It can be found here. I like the layout of the site. Initially, I wanted my website to look like this, all on one page. However, in the end I found it to be a little much. What I don’t like about it is when you’re on a section such as “about” or “resume”, you have to scroll through the entire section to be able procceed to the next section of the webpage.

  • I was also inspired by the Maya Nelson layout on Wix, which can be found here. I like the layout and design of the information in this example. It almost appears to be in the form of post cards that align in the centre of the page. What I don’t like, however, is the colour scheme. It’s a bit too bland. Initially, I thought it might be a somewhat soothing colour, but I want viewers to be energised and excited by my work, so I needed some colour that would be more active.
