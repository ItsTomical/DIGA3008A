Pizza Eats Game

Outsmart the dice, outdeliver your rival. In this clever 2D roll-and-write game, your success depends on how you play the hand—or rather, the dice—you’re dealt. Each turn, three numbers shape your strategy as you customize toppings, select pizza slices, and navigate a lively neighborhood to complete deliveries. It’s a fast-paced race to serve every house before your opponent, but with luck and logic constantly colliding, no two rounds are ever the same. Can you stay one step ahead and deliver the final slice?

Play on Itch.io

Air Hockey

This classic air hockey game pits you against an AI opponent in a fast-paced digital match — but with a unique twist: randomly spawning portals on the board add chaos and strategy to every rally. Built as part of a physics-focused assignment, the game incorporates real-world collision logic using Unity’s 2D physics system.
